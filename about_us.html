About Us
PT Enesers Mitra Berkah is a PMDN company that located in Taman Tekno Blok K2 No 12, Bumi Serpong Damai, South Tangerang, Banten Indonesia. Our company moves in the field of manufacturing local medical devices with specialists in the field electromedicine-based medical devices. PT Enesers Mitra Berkah was established on November 25, 2016 by taking the momentum of the issuance of the presidential instruction of the Republic of Indonesia No. 6 Th 2016 signed by President Joko Widodo on the 8th June 2016 on the acceleration of the pharmaceutical and medical device industry, which is the government's support for local industries pharmaceuticals and medical devices. Establishment of Enesers Mitra Berkah completes the Partner company Other medical device groups. PT Enesers Mitra Berkah will became one of the manufacturing companies that implemented medium to high-tech technology in the field of medical devices, to realize the independence of the domestic industry.
